  • AWS CloudFront is a CDN that delivers content globally with low latency by caching it in edge locations.
  • It integrates with AWS services like S3 and EC2 to serve both static and dynamic content.
  • CloudFront supports SSL/TLS for secure transmission and offers built-in protection through AWS Shield and WAF.
  • It automatically scales to meet traffic demands and operates on a pay-as-you-go model.
